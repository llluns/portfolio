Hello!

I'm Lauren Lunsford

Welcome to my Website!

I am a Senior Product Analyst at 23andMe. Previously, I was a Masters student at the University of Michigan studying Information Science with an emphasis on Big Data Analytics and Human-Computer Interaction. I like people and love understanding behavior through qualitative and quantitative data to see how we can design a world that is a little bit better. Sometimes, I also like to make art. This is a website to archive all the projects I have completed.

About Me

Data

I am incredibly fascinated by how individuals and groups of people make decisions. I use data science and qualitative methods to understand behavior and believe that if we can understand these processes, we can build products and establish strategies that achieve both business and user needs.

Art

As with my academic interests, I love exploring aspects of humanity and people through my art. A common theme throughout my pieces is the exploration of the human form and how our interpretations change with different artistic elements.

Baking

I love love sugar and taught myself how to bake to feed that addiction. Baking is another creative outlet that lets me try out different flavors, fail, and improve. It is also a mechanism through which I get to connect with people, by cooking with friends and family or saying thank you to an advisor with a macaron.
