Hello!

I'm Lauren Lunsford

Welcome to my Website!

I am a Masters student at the University of Michigan studying Information Science with an emphasis on Big Data Analytics and Human-Computer Interaction. I like people and love understanding behavior from the perspective of cognitive science, economics, and data to see how we can design a world that is a little bit better. Sometimes, I also like to make art. This is a website to archive all the projects I have completed.

❮ ❯

People

I am incredibly fascinated by how individuals and groups of people make decisions. I use data science and qualitative methods to understand behavior and believe that if we can understand these processes, we can structure policy, products, and our environments to help people make the best decisions for themselves and for others.

Data

As humans generate exponentially more data, I believe it is imperative to understand how to handle and interpret it. Data, together with industry knowledge, can shed light on how people behave in specific scenarios which is crucial information when building an effective solution to any problem.

Baking

I love love sugar and taught myself how to bake to feed that addiction. Baking is another creative outlet that lets me try out different flavors, fail, and improve. It is also a mechanism through which I get to connect with people, by cooking with friends and family or saying thank you to an advisor with a macaron.

Art

As with my academic interests, I love exploring aspects of humanity and people through my art. A common theme throughout my pieces is the exploration of the human form and how our interpretations change with different artistic elements.
